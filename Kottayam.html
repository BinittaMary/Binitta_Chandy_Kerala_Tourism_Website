Kottayam

About Kottayam

Kottayam is an important trading centre of commercial crops, panoramic backwater stretches, lush paddy fields, high lands extensive rubber plantations and total literate people which have given this district the enviable title ‘ the land of letters, latex and lakes’. Kottayam is also the first city in to launch an ‘Eco city project’. Kottayam has played major role in all the political agitations of modern times. The ‘Malayali Memorial’ which sought to secure better representation for educated Travancoreans in the Travancore civil service against person from outside may be said to have had its orgin in kottayam.

Places to Visit in Kottayam

Kumarakom

Kumarakom is a sleepy little village on VembanadLake in Kerala. It offers wide variety of flora, exotic sightseeing, boating and fishing experience. VemanadLake is home to several types of marine life exclusively the Chemeen (Prawns), the Konju (Tiger Prawns) and Karimeem.

Elaveezhapoonchira

Elaveezhapoonchira means valley where leaves dont fall and is names so because the place has no trees. This is also one of the best places in Kerala to witness both the sun rise and sunset.

Illikkal Kallu

Illikkal Kallu is a major tourist attraction and one of the most pristine spots in all of India. Situated in Moonnilavu Village, Meenachil taluk, the nearest city to the summit is Teekoy. Besides several other factors which make this peak unique, the most prominent feature is that half the boulder of the majestic rock has fallen apart.

Marmala Waterfalls

Blessed with a rich biodiversity, an emerald forest cover and an all around utopian environment, the waterfalls is the perfect place to get away from the din and chaos of the city and spend some moments of solitude to reinvigorate and rejuvenate.
